My Story

As a dedicated registered nurse with a comprehensive background in healthcare, I commenced my nursing career in 2021, navigating through the challenges of a global pandemic to complete the majority of my nursing education. Specializing in diverse clinical areas, including cardiac vascular step-down, labor and delivery, and postpartum care, I've garnered invaluable experience in delivering high-quality patient-centered services.

My professional journey took an unexpected turn with my introduction to coding through SheCodes, where I participated in a workshop that ignited an unforeseen passion for programming. Discovering not only an aptitude but genuine enjoyment in crafting and optimizing code to achieve desired web page aesthetics, I realized the profound satisfaction that coding brought me, unlike anything I had experienced before.

Motivated by this newfound passion, I am pursuing a transition from bedside nursing to a career in coding, aspiring to secure a role as a front-end developer. The constant learning and problem-solving aspects of coding captivate me, pushing me to acquire proficiency in various programming languages. My aspirations extend to becoming a full-stack developer, broadening my skill set to encompass both front-end and back-end technologies. My commitment to continuous learning is reflected in my pursuit of completing all SheCodes workshops, ensuring a robust foundation for my professional journey in the dynamic field of web development.
